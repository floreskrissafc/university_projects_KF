KOIOS

Try on a iOS Simulator

Description

Embark on a knowledge journey with KOIOS. Tailored for English and Spanish learners, KOIOS offers a structured approach through CEFR levels, from A1 to C2. Earn badges, collect points, and delight in charming visuals reminiscent of ancient Greek landscapes. Points accumulate with each correct word, fostering a competitive yet enriching learning experience. Track your progress with insightful stats, including daily comparisons, streak days, and usage time. Personalize your learning by marking words as known, ensuring a targeted and efficient learning path. KOIOS makes language learning dynamic, rewarding, and tailored to your individual progress.
