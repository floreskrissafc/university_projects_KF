Description

Embark on an intergalactic adventure with this p5.js rendition of the classic Asteroids game. Navigate through the cosmos in your spaceship, deftly dodging asteroids as you unleash a barrage of shots to protect Earth. The spaceship responds to your every command, firing thrusters with arrow keys that leave a fiery trail in their wake. Engage in epic asteroid battles, complete with explosive animations and satisfying sound effects. As you skillfully destroy asteroids, watch the counter climb, but beware of Earth's atmosphere—entering its gravitational pull can spell disaster! It's a nostalgic journey through space, skill, and strategy, where every move counts in the quest to save Earth.
