Bouncing Balls

Play the Game

Description

Dive into a colorful world of bouncing balls with this p5.js creation. Click to release vibrant balls that gracefully fall and playfully bounce off the floor. Each ball is a burst of random color, bouncing at its own unique pace until its energy fades away. It's a visual symphony of gravity and randomness, where every click brings a delightful array of bouncing hues.
