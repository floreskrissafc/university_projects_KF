Venezuela Loot Tracker

The Venezuela Loot Tracker is an interactive database powered by the Venezuelan Asset Recovery Initiative (INRAV) that enables tracking of the list of assets forfeited by U.S. courts in corruption cases involving Venezuelan officials linked to the governments of Hugo Chávez and Nicolás Maduro.

The information in the Venezuela Loot Tracker was generated in partnership with Transparencia Venezuela and is based on research from the Corruptómetro.

As of 2024, there is a total of 72 legal cases opened by the Department of Justice.
