Angry Birds Clone

Play the Game

Description

Step into a world of physics and cuteness with this charming Angry Birds game clone. Armed with a slingshot, unleash adorable birds to topple a tower of boxes. But there's a twist – a playful propeller adds a dynamic element. Adjust its speed to strategize unique bird trajectories and send them soaring into the chaos. Powered by Matter.js and p5.js, the game intricately simulates the physics of collisions and restitution coefficients, creating a delightful blend of strategy, physics, and cuteness.
