Plinko

Play the Game

Description

Enjoy a casual game of Plinko, brought to life with p5.js and Matter.js! Drop balls and watch them bounce through the pegs, adding a touch of randomness to your day. It's a playful blend of code and physics for a fun and interactive experience. Give it a try, embrace the simplicity, and let the balls find their own paths!
