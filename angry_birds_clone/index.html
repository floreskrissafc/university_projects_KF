Click and hold the pink bird to aim it towards the boxes. Release the mouse to throw the bird.

Press the Left Arrow to increase the angular velocity of the bar propeller. Press the Right Arrow to decrease its speed.

Press the "r" key to set up the pink bird back in the slingshot.

Press the "b" to release a yellow bird at any position where your cursor is. Use the propeller to throw these birds at the boxes.

You win the game if you can remove all the boxes from the canvas before the time runs out.
