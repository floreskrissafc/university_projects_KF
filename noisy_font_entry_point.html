Description

Explore the fun side of code with this p5.js project. Using p5.js, the contours of the word 'code' dance and sway, responding to your every move. As the mouse glides, the letters playfully waver. Simple and charming, it's a delightful showcase of how a touch of interactivity can add whimsy to the world of coding. Enjoy the playful dance of letters in this creative exploration.
